Strategy: Dual Moving Average Crossover (Golden Cross / Death Cross)
Rules:
- Buy (Golden Cross): 50-day Simple Moving Average (SMA) crosses above the 200-day SMA.
- Sell (Death Cross): 50-day SMA crosses below the 200-day SMA.
Timeframe: Daily / Weekly (Swing Trading)
Market: Major Equities (SPY, QQQ) and Forex (EUR/USD)
Risk Assumption: Moderate volatility, large capitalization focus.

Goal: Provide a comprehensive logic audit of the trend-following mechanism and identify historical fail-points during ranging markets.
